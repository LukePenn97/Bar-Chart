About This Project

This is a project for the Lighthouse Labs web development bootcamp, starting on March 29 2021.
I started working on this prep project on March 20th, and it is now March 27th at the time of writing.
I am happy with my work, and I am looking forward to the main course. I am most proud of the build a chart page,
as it was not required for the project, and I think it turned out well.
